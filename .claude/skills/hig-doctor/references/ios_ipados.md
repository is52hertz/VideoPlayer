# iOS & iPadOS Design Guidelines for VideoPlayer

## iOS (iPhone)
- **Touch-First**: Design for direct manipulation.
- **Handheld Ergonomics**: Place controls in the middle or bottom area for easy reach.
- **Concentration**: Limit onscreen controls to focus on the video. Use tap-to-reveal patterns.
- **Orientation**: Seamlessly adapt to portrait and landscape (immersive).

## iPadOS
- **Expanded Canvas**: Use sidebars or multi-column layouts for libraries (if added).
- **Multitasking**: Support Split View, Slide Over, and Stage Manager.
- **Input Diversity**: Support touch, pointer (trackpad/mouse), keyboard, and Apple Pencil.
- **Adaptation**: Transition effortlessly between handheld and "laptop-like" (macOS-lite) modes.

## Common Best Practices
- Adapt to Dark Mode and Dynamic Type.
- Use native materials (vibrancy/blur) for overlays.
- Respect safe areas to avoid clipping content.
